About Us

Our Story

Vibrant Impact Ministries (VIM) is a Holy Spirit-filled prophetic and teaching church founded by Prophet Valentine Ifedayo in 2020. What began as a small gathering in Akure, Ondo State, has grown into a vibrant community impacting lives across Nigeria and beyond.

Since our inception, we have witnessed countless testimonies of salvation, healing, deliverance, and supernatural transformation. Through both on-site and online services, we continue to reach souls globally with the message of Christ and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Spiritual Leader

Prophet Valentine Ifedayo

Founder & Senior Prophet

Prophet Valentine Ifedayo is a Nigerian prophet, teacher, and revivalist known for accurate prophetic declarations, powerful healing and deliverance ministry, and a burning passion for souls.

His ministry emphasizes faith, restoration, and awakening spiritual gifts in believers. Through the anointing of the Holy Spirit, he has helped countless individuals experience divine transformation, breakthrough, and fulfillment of their God-given destiny.

Prophet Valentine is committed to raising a generation of believers who walk in the supernatural and impact their world for Christ.
